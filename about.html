What is Travelue?

Travelue is a travel agency based in Hartford, Connecticut that has been serving customers for over 5 years. Our founder Cierra Richardson, holds a Bachelors degree in Business Administration and a minor in Travel and Tourism and has been leading this company to success. So far, we have helped plan our customers the ultimate vacation experiences in places such as Brazil, Jamaica, and Italy. We look forward to helping you plan your next dream vacation!
